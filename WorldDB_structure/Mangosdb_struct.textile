h3. This is the list of tables in the regular 'mangos' database

(Reloadable) - Reloadable in-game with the .reload command.
(CMaNGOS) - CMaNGOS table. These tables are either used by MaNGOS internally, or they are maintained by the CMaNGOS team. Either way, the DB teams do not touch them.
(ScriptLibrary) - Script library tables. These tables are maintained by script library projects (such as ScriptDev2). The DB teams do not touch them.
